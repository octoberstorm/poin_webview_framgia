Story of Poin

Our inspiration for Poin comes from everyday life in Tokyo, where over 160,000 restaurants (close to 10 times more than in New York,) millions of products, and thousands of shops and brands, all compete for our attention using everything from traditional advertising to social media to feed us information, non-stop.

With seemingly infinite choices, we wanted a “filter” to make discovery simple.

And, we realized that

オタク
are all around us to help us "filter" the world:

Otaku
[おたく/オタク] noun
Otaku is a Japanese term used to refer to people who obsessive interests.
by wikipedia
  • A friend infatuated with wine.
  • A colleague passionate about art and museums.
  • Classmates with terrific tastes in fashion.

We’re all “otaku” of something: from hobbies we’re obsessively into, to little daily things we experience firsthand.

So, we started building Poin, a tool to make your interests useful & beautiful, and to see the world through the lens of people’s interests.

Start a book. Write what you’re interested in. And make someone’s life better.

Follow your interests.
